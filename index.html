Unbeaten Tenbury March On:

11th January 2020

Tenbury RFC got the new year off to a flying start with our biggest win of the season so far - 81-0 against Dixonians which keeps us four points clear at the top of the table. Next up is Stourport away on Saturday, KO 2.15pm if you can make it over to support. Actually, we are taking both sides over - Firsts and Seconds - so there will be a feast of rugby on offer.

Tenbury Break Losing Duck:

8th November 2019

Tenbury had their forward pack to thank for a well worked first victory in the Midlands 4 West (South) league. A hard fought first half, with plenty of opportunities created but none taken left Tenbury 0-7 down at half time. A rousing team talk by Andrew Black and Jon Males gave the impetus for Tenbury to kick on.

Tenbury Raring To Go For New Season:

4th September 2019

Tenbury arrived at Droitwich with a full squad and a good bank of preseason training behind them. Tenbury played an excellent game, holding their own against a larger pack which allowed the backs to create space and gaps to break through and put Droitwich to the sword. Tenbury ran in 7 tries in total highlighted by hatrick for both Callum Spires and Hobie Gill. Luke Gill added the final try to end the game 41-0 to Tenbury.
